About us

Welcome to Game Hub; The Universe of games enterprises. We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the environment and having an ethical work relationship with our developers.

We are a 100% digital based enterprise and therefore the need of finding new ways to use renewable energy to cool down our 20,000 square feet data senter. Using hydroelectric and wind power for this purpose covers our needs and beyond.

Been a 100% digital based enterprise also demand a developer team that covers all of our challenges and/or needs. We believe on ensuring fair trade by upholding ethical labour practices, providing competitive compensation, equal growth opportunities and a healthy work-life balance.
